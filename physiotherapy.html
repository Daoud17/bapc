Physiotherapy: Everything You Need to Know

If you are experiencing acute pain (re: sudden), chronic pain (re: ongoing), or having difficulties with mobility and physical movement, physiotherapy can help.

Physiotherapy is the process of helping individuals restore, maintain, and improve their physical health and mobility.

Physiotherapy is commonly used to relieve:

  • Back and neck pain
  • Shoulder, elbow, and wrist pain
  • Hip, knee, and ankle pain
  • Sport injuries
  • Motor Vehicle accidents
  • Fractures, sprains and strains
  • Arthritis
  • Repetitive use injuries
  • Post-operative care and rehab
  • Pre and post-surgery rehabilitation
  • Concussions
  • Pelvic conditions

If you are suffering from any of the above or anything similar, talk with us. We’ll help you uncover next steps for treatment and relief.

What You Can Expect from Beirut Advanced Physiotherapy Center

We specialize in providing fast, effective care when you need it most. Here’s how we work with patients to understand their needs, reduce pain, and support lasting recovery:

Interview

First, we speak directly with you to understand your symptoms, goals, daily activities, and any previous injuries or medical history that may affect your recovery.

Assessment

Next, we perform a detailed physical assessment to evaluate movement, strength, flexibility, posture, and functional limitations.

Diagnosis

Based on your assessment, we identify the underlying cause of your pain or limitation and explain it clearly so you understand what’s happening.

Treatment recommendations and planning

We create a personalized short- and long-term plan, including a schedule of sessions and the right techniques to support recovery, prevent re-injury, and improve overall function.

Exercise education

You’ll receive guided exercises and practical advice you can do safely at home to keep improving between sessions and speed up progress.

Functional / activity-level progressions

As you improve, we update your plan to match your goals — whether that’s returning to sport, work, or daily activities — with measurable milestones and ongoing support at every step.
